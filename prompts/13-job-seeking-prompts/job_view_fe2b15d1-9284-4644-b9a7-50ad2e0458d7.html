About the Role

Adaptify is building the future of AI-powered SEO at scale. We're looking for a vibe coder who can ship fast, think creatively, and build AI-driven tools that help businesses rank and grow organically. You'll work across the full stack — from LLM integrations and content pipelines to frontend dashboards and analytics. We move fast, ship daily, and trust our team to own their work. This is a fully remote, async-first role. No standups, no micromanagement — just great work.

Requirements

  • -Experience building with LLMs (Claude, GPT, etc.)
  • -Strong full-stack skills (TypeScript, React/Next.js, Python, Node.js)
  • -Comfortable with databases (PostgreSQL, Supabase)
  • -Self-directed and comfortable working async
  • -Bonus: SEO knowledge or interest in search/content
