About the job

Elite engineers don't just write code—they weaponize AI to make traditional development obsolete. If you're still debugging manually while others are deploying autonomously, you're already behind.

At Trilogy, we've eliminated the gap between AI potential and engineering reality. Our development ecosystem doesn't merely incorporate AI tools—it's fundamentally built around them. While most teams debate whether to adopt Copilot, our engineers are orchestrating AI agents to detect defects before they occur, auto-generate solutions, and optimize performance at scale.

This role demands an engineer who can dismantle legacy B2B products and reconstruct them as AI-enhanced cloud-native applications. You'll architect systems where every component—from feature ideation to deployment—leverages advanced AI capabilities like retrieval-augmented generation. Your mandate: zero defects, zero downtime, zero compromises. If you prefer architectural debates over shipped products, look elsewhere.

What You Will Be Doing


Harness advanced RAG vector stores and AI analysis tools to preemptively identify, diagnose, and eliminate product defects with precision and speed
Architect and deploy AI development agents that autonomously design and implement innovative features for enterprise-grade cloud-native applications
Drive collaboration across global engineering teams to deliver scalable solutions that exceed enterprise quality standards


What You Won’t Be Doing


Managing infrastructure overhead: Our AI-optimized DevOps eliminates the burden of routine configuration and maintenance tasks
Sacrificing development time for excessive meetings: We maintain a strict engineering-focused environment where implementation trumps discussion
Writing manual test cases: Our AI testing framework automates test generation, execution, and validation
Building features without AI augmentation: Every component you develop will leverage advanced AI capabilities


Vibe Coder / Software Engineer Key Responsibilities

Transform service provider capabilities by implementing sophisticated AI-driven features that eliminate workflow inefficiencies and deliver measurable business impact.

Basic Requirements


4+ years of proven commercial software development experience, specifically building production-grade server-side web applications
Demonstrated proficiency with GenAI code assistants (Github Copilot, Cursor, v0.dev) in professional development environments
Commitment to integrating GenAI tools throughout your development workflow to maximize efficiency and output quality


About Trilogy

Hundreds of software businesses run on the Trilogy Business Platform. For three decades, Trilogy has been known for 3 things: Relentlessly seeking top talent, Innovating new technology, and incubating new businesses. Our technological innovation is spearheaded by a passion for simple customer-facing designs. Our incubation of new businesses ranges from entirely new moon-shot ideas to rearchitecting existing projects for today's modern cloud-based stack. Trilogy is a place where you can be surrounded with great people, be proud of doing great work, and grow your career by leaps and bounds.

There is so much to cover for this exciting role, and space here is limited. Hit the Apply button if you found this interesting and want to learn more. We look forward to meeting you!

Working with us

This is a full-time (40 hours per week), long-term position. The position is immediately available and requires entering into an independent contractor agreement with Crossover as a Contractor of Record. The compensation level for this role is $30 USD/hour, which equates to $60,000 USD/year assuming 40 hours per week and 50 weeks per year. The payment period is weekly. Consult www.crossover.com/help-and-faqs for more details on this topic.
