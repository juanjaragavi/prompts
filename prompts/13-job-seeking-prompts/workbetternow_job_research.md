## Full Job Description

The AI Software Developer designs, builds, and ships internal applications that streamline operations, reduce software licensing costs, and scale with business growth. Using AI-assisted development tools (Replit, Claude Code, Copilot), you'll rapidly prototype and deliver production-ready tools that replace selected third-party software and manual workflows. This role combines hands-on engineering with a product mindset, turning business problems into working software quickly, securely, and cost-effectively.

### Key Responsibilities

- Build, ship, and maintain internal web applications using AI-assisted development environments, translating business requirements into working software quickly while maintaining reliability and security standards.
- Partner with leadership to identify high-cost or underused software that can be replaced with purpose-built internal tools, and estimate cost savings delivered by each replacement project.
- Write clean, documented, and maintainable code following source control and deployment best practices; apply security and data protection standards to all internal tools.
- Work directly with stakeholders across departments to scope, prioritize, and demo new tools; maintain transparent backlog communication and document each tool thoroughly for organizational continuity.
- Stay current on AI development tools and techniques, establish and report on delivery KPIs (tools shipped, cost savings, adoption, uptime), and propose new opportunities for internal tooling.

### Requirements

- Live in LATAM or The Caribbean.
- 3+ years of experience building and shipping web applications with a track record of delivering working software quickly.
- Fluent in English (written and spoken) – able to communicate effectively with US-based teams and stakeholders.
- Hands-on experience with AI-assisted development tools (Replit, Claude Code, Copilot, GitHub Copilot, or similar).
- Proficiency in modern web stack: JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Node.js, Python.
- Experience building applications that connect to third-party SaaS platforms via REST APIs.
- Proven experience working in Scrum/Agile frameworks with sprint planning, standups, retrospectives, and iterative delivery.
- Sound judgment on build-versus-buy decisions and ability to prioritize work for business impact.

### Technical Requirements

- Processor: Intel 11th Gen (or AMD Ryzen 5000 series) for Windows | M2 for Macs.
- RAM: 16 GB minimum.
- Internet: Stable connection of 25-50 Mbps minimum.

### Preferred

- Experience replacing paid SaaS tools with custom-built internal applications.
- Familiarity with HubSpot, Microsoft 365, SharePoint, or Zoho Recruit APIs.
- Understanding of data privacy, security, and compliance fundamentals.
- Hands-on containerization experience (Docker and/or Kubernetes) in cloud environments.

- A supportive, people-first environment where your voice matters.
- Two in-person team gatherings per year, hosted in your local city.
- Training and development support to keep your skills sharp and current.
