About the job

We're looking for a Vibe Coder — a creative builder who thrives at the intersection of code, AI, and culture.

As a Vibe Coder, you'll shape the way our teams work by designing tools, automations, and AI-powered utilities that make internal workflows smoother, faster, and more fun. You'll prototype new ideas quickly, experiment fearlessly, and bring a hacker's mindset to enhancing human creativity and productivity.

What You'll Do

  • Design, build, and maintain internal tools, automations, and AI integrations that empower teams and improve workflows.
  • Prototype new AI utilities and productivity features that let humans focus on human things — creativity, strategy, and collaboration.
  • Partner with cross-functional teams to discover pain points, design AI-driven solutions, and measure their impact.
  • Experiment with emerging tools and frameworks, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with vibe coding platforms and AI coding assistants.
  • Translate ambiguous problems into tangible prototypes, testing and iterating quickly to validate ideas.
  • Contribute to a culture of playful innovation, helping make technology feel approachable, inspiring, and even a little magical

Requirements

  • Experience building products — whether through startups, side projects, open source contributions, or shipping tools inside a company.
  • Experience working with vibe coding tools such as v0, Bolt, Lovable, or Chef by Convex.
  • Experience working with AI coding editors (e.g., Cursor) to speed up development and experimentation.
  • A creative, curious mindset: you love tinkering, prototyping, and bringing abstract ideas to life fast.
  • A collaborative spirit: you enjoy partnering with non-technical teams to uncover needs and build impactful solutions.
  • Strong problem-solving skills: you're comfortable moving from vague problem spaces to concrete, usable products

Benefits

  • Language Classes: Access to language classes (English, Portuguese, Spanish) to enhance communication skills
  • OpenAI Premium License: Complimentary access to an OpenAI premium license for personal or professional use
  • Paid Time Off: Enjoy 25 days/year of paid vacations and holidays to recharge and maintain a healthy work-life balance
  • Soft Hybrid Work: We meet 1 days/week in our Co Work offices, the rest of the time you can work remotely from wherever you like!
