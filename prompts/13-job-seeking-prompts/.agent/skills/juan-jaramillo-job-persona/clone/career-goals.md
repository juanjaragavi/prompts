# Career Goals

## Current Stage

Juan Jaramillo is in an active job search while continuing to operate as an independent AI/ML consultant. This stage is not a reset; it is a positioning phase to convert years of technical leadership, entrepreneurial execution, and Generative AI specialization into the next high-leverage role.

## Primary Objective

The main goal is to secure a senior-level role that combines:

- AI/ML leadership
- Hands-on technical depth
- Product and systems thinking
- Real ownership over architecture and delivery
- Strong compensation aligned with impact
- Flexibility for remote or international collaboration

## Target Role Categories

Priority role types include:

- AI Engineering Lead
- AI Development Lead
- Senior GenAI Engineer
- LLM Engineer
- AI Solutions Architect
- Senior Machine Learning Engineer with GenAI focus
- AI-Native Full-Stack Architect
- Forward Deployed AI Engineer
- Senior AI Product Engineer

## Ideal Positioning

Juan should be positioned as an:

- AI-native full-stack architect
- Senior AI/ML operator
- Generative AI specialist with production delivery experience
- Builder who can move from strategy to implementation
- Technical leader who understands business outcomes

This positioning is stronger than presenting him as only a researcher, only a developer, or only a consultant.

## Core Career Themes

The strongest long-term career themes are:

- Enterprise Generative AI implementation
- LLM fine-tuning and optimization
- Agentic systems and orchestration
- AI-native SaaS product design
- Full-stack AI applications
- AI workflow automation
- Technical leadership for applied AI teams
- Responsible and practical AI adoption

## Near-Term Goals

Over the next 3 to 6 months, Juan should focus on:

- Landing a senior AI/ML or AI-native engineering role
- Strengthening public proof of execution through case studies and portfolio assets
- Refining job-search materials for target role tracks
- Expanding visibility as a credible AI systems builder
- Turning past work into reusable professional narratives

## Medium-Term Goals

Over the next 1 to 2 years, the goal is to:

- Lead or co-lead high-impact AI product and engineering initiatives
- Own system design for production AI platforms
- Deepen authority in LLM evaluation, fine-tuning, orchestration, and AI product delivery
- Increase compensation, influence, and strategic autonomy
- Build a stronger public reputation in applied enterprise AI

## Long-Term Goals

Longer term, Juan should aim to:

- Operate as a recognized AI/ML leader with a distinct market profile
- Build or help scale high-value AI products, platforms, or consulting offerings
- Create a durable professional brand around pragmatic AI leadership
- Maintain a hybrid identity: strategist, architect, and builder
- Develop assets, systems, and frameworks that compound over time

## Non-Negotiables

Strong-fit opportunities should include most of the following:

- Real technical ownership
- Respect for quality and architecture
- Practical use of AI, not superficial experimentation
- Strategic exposure, not just ticket execution
- A modern engineering environment
- Clear room for influence and growth

Avoid roles that are:

- Purely managerial with no technical depth
- Vague about AI scope or maturity
- Misaligned on compensation
- Built around hype rather than production value
- Dependent on weak engineering culture

## Role Selection Heuristics

Prefer roles where:

- The company has a real AI use case
- There is visible product or operational leverage
- Juan can contribute across architecture, systems, and implementation
- Technical leadership is valued
- The role benefits from both his AI expertise and full-stack experience

When evaluating roles, ask:

1. Is the AI work real or superficial?
2. Is there room to lead, not just execute?
3. Will this role strengthen long-term positioning?
4. Does the stack and scope match Juan’s strengths?
5. Is the opportunity worth the time and attention required?

## Geographic and Market Preferences

Preferred opportunities include:

- Remote roles based in the U.S. or global markets
- Colombia-based remote roles
- LatAm regional opportunities with strong compensation
- Select opportunities in cities such as Bogotá, Medellín, Mexico City, or Buenos Aires

The best opportunities are remote-friendly, high-trust, and results-oriented.

## Compensation Intent

Compensation should reflect:

- Seniority

Juan should optimize for high-leverage roles where compensation aligns with the value of advanced AI/ML and full-stack leadership rather than commodity development work.
