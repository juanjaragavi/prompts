# Templates: Recruiter Outreach Responses

Canned building blocks for LinkedIn InMail and WhatsApp replies. Use
these as a starting point, then personalize to the specific recruiter
message. Always apply the Tone Rules and Attachment Protocol from
SKILL.md.

## English Templates

### Opener (first contact, InMail)
> Hi [Recruiter Name], thank you for reaching out! I'm very
> interested in learning more about the [Role] position.

### Positioning statement
> With 17+ years in digital/AI initiatives -- most recently as AI
> Development Lead at TopNetworks Inc., where I built enterprise
> GenAI SaaS tools (Next.js, Vertex AI, PostgreSQL) -- I believe
> there's strong alignment with this role.

### Attachment line (first substantive reply only)
> I'm attaching my CV, cover letter, and services portfolio for your
> review -- happy to answer any questions.

### Closing / call-to-action
> Would you have 20 minutes this week for a call? I'm based in
> Bogota, Colombia (GMT-5), and can be flexible with timing.

### Full example -- LinkedIn InMail (first contact, impersonated)
> Hi [Recruiter Name], thank you for reaching out! I'm very
> interested in learning more about the [Role] position. With 17+
> years in digital/AI initiatives -- most recently as AI Development
> Lead at TopNetworks Inc., where I built enterprise GenAI SaaS tools
> (Next.js, Vertex AI, PostgreSQL) -- I believe there's strong
> alignment. I'm attaching my CV, cover letter, and services
> portfolio for your review. Would you have 20 minutes this week for
> a call? I'm based in Bogota (GMT-5). Best, Juan

### Full example -- WhatsApp (follow-up, impersonated)
> Hi [Name], thanks for the update! Tuesday 10am (Bogota time) works
> well for me. Looking forward to it. Best, Juan

### Deferring compensation questions
> I'd prefer to discuss compensation in more detail on a call, once
> we've covered the role's scope -- but I'm generally open and
> flexible depending on the full package.

### Requesting more info
> Could you share the job description or more details about the
> team structure? That will help me speak more specifically to how
> my experience fits.

## Spanish Templates (Plantillas en Espanol)

### Apertura (primer contacto)
> Hola [Nombre], ¡muchas gracias por escribirme! Me interesa mucho
> conocer más sobre la vacante de [Rol].

### Declaracion de posicionamiento
> Cuento con más de 17 años de experiencia en iniciativas
> digitales y de IA -- recientemente como AI Development Lead en
> TopNetworks Inc., donde diseñé herramientas SaaS de GenAI
> empresarial (Next.js, Vertex AI, PostgreSQL) -- por lo que creo que
> hay una gran afinidad con esta posición.

### Linea de adjuntos (solo primera respuesta sustantiva)
> Adjunto mi CV, carta de presentación y portafolio de servicios
> para tu revisión -- quedo atento a cualquier pregunta.

### Cierre / llamado a la accion
> ¿Tendrías 20 minutos esta semana para una llamada? Estoy en
> Bogotá, Colombia (GMT-5) y puedo ajustarme a tu disponibilidad.

### Ejemplo completo -- WhatsApp (seguimiento, personificado)
> Hola [Nombre], ¡gracias por la actualización! El martes a las
> 10am (hora Bogotá) me queda perfecto. Quedo atento. Saludos, Juan

## Usage Notes
- Always replace bracketed placeholders before sending.
- Never send a message containing unresolved placeholders like
  [Role] or [Recruiter Name].
- Match the recruiter's language; do not switch languages mid-thread
  unless they do first.
